In order to increase the variety of the farming business, the host created the activity of “salted duck eggs DIY”, which uses ancient producing methods! After reserving in advance, the host would prepare red soil mud, which is a mixing of red soil containing minerals from Mountain Tatu, salt and hot boiled water by 3:1:1 proportion.
By covering the egg with the red soil mud, salt and minerals would penetrate in the duck eggs through the pore on the shell. Such eggs would be ready to eat after 20 days at normal temperature. If you like it saltier, you can wait 30 days before eating them.
The hostess explained the process of manufacturing carefully for us.
These were duck eggs prepared, and the host added the manufacture date on them.
The red soil mud should cover the whole egg.
Everybody enjoyed making salty duck eggs.
