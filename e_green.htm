Green building
“Architectures whose construction materials and various facilities follow the design concepts of environmental protection, sustainable resources, and energy conservation” can be referred to as “Green Architectures.” The White Horse Farm is a Mediterranean-style building designed with the Green Architectures concept in mind. It utilizes environmentally friendly construction materials combined with environmentally friendly design concepts. In terms of ventilation and sunlight, the design of the building focuses on saving energy. It also uses many recycled materials for its décor. Come visit the White Horse Farm, and you can personally experience and admire the owners’ ingenuity and efforts in make such an environmentally friendly architecture.
The overall structure uses paint that has the “Green Certification Label,” which means that it does not contain any heavy metals, is UV resistant, does not contain toxins, and is durable and long-lasting. The blue movable shutters provide good ventilation, allows an ample amount of sunlight to come through, are good for also blocking the sunlight when you need to, and provide anti-theft features.

Many environmentally friendly concepts were also used when designing the indoor and outdoor environments of the farm, and many recycled objects and materials were used for decoration.
Recycled oak barrels were used to make bar platforms, wine barrel display tables, stereo system cabinets, as well as computer desks.
Recycled wine barrels and clamp pots were used for landscaping
Recycled wine bottles were used to form retaining walls.
Recycled railroad ties were used to make chairs.
Recycled plastic buckets were used to form separation walls.
Old recycled chairs were re-painted and reused.
Left-over materials from the oak barrels were used to make a bulletin board.
Recycled sewing machines were used to make dining tables.
Recycled wood materials were used to make a DIY mailbox.
Recycled medicine cabinets from the Health Center were used as coffee cabinets.
And their grass lawns are mowed by “automatic lawn mowers” – horses.
A left-over wall from the demolition of a building was painted and used as the entrance sign to the farm.
