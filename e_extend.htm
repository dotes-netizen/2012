We went to visit another horse farmer recommended by the Lin’s Horse Farm’s owner. It is located in Hsienhsi, Changhua County, too. There, what surprised us most were the spacious horse farm, a wide, green lawn, comfortable horse houses, and available warehouse. From those, we know that he is definitely an expert of breeding horses.

First, the expert introduced the living surroundings of the horses. He emphasized the horse houses are bigger than any others in Taiwan. The horses lived not only in the big houses, but also on plenty of sea sand for sunbathing. As for the sea sand, the expert explained that it is to prevent the horses from skin disease. The expert has taken care of and schooled horses since 1987. It has already been 24 years. In addition to caring and schooling, the expert also know how to make horse riding stunt, jumping.
Later on, the expert introduced the horses for us. There are four mini horses, and one big horse. The big one is Breton from New Zealand, while the mini ones are from Taiwan. With regard to the price of horses, the expert told us the big one is about six hundred thousand; the mini ones are approximately one hundred thousands NT dollars.

The expert is really worthy of the name because he can take care of the horses, and do the shoeing. What’s more, he can cure his horses when they are ill and sick. Afterwards, we asked the expert how to ride a horse. He mentioned there are some basic equipments such as a saddle, lasso (to control the directions), knee braces (prevent from injury), winker (to concentrate on), horseshoes (to protect feet). When riding on a horse, you should move in rhythm, and then transit from a slower to a faster pace. Besides, if you pull the right rein, the horse will turn to the right, and vice versa. To stop the horses, you should pull up the reins.
The host rode horses.(Note 1)

Subsequently, the expert taught us how he cares the horses every day. In the early morning, he picks the hooves, feeds them, and uses a variety of brushes to do grooming. He said trimming is needless because the horses will remove loose winter hair in spring. For feeding the horses, it’s best to feed them three times daily. There is something interesting, that is, they must eat the same every meal, or they would refuse to eat. In addition, the dinner should include forages such as grass or hay. Alfalfa and carrot are also good food for them.

Those horses work on weekends in a scenic spot in Miaoli. What they do is ridden by visitors. The expert said “I wash the horses once a week. Here are the steps. I apply shampoo to their hair, and brush it. Later, I wash them in cold water. Oh! Don’t worry. Those horses don’t easily get ill because they are cold-hardy animals. Nevertheless, if the weather is too cold for them to bear, I wouldn’t do the washing. Or probably, they could catch a cold. Concerning catching a cold, the expert told us how to know the sickness of a horse. As a matter of fact, when a horse looks impotent, weak, it must be sick. At that time, the expert would ask a veterinarian for help, and comfort it. Besides, due to the heat in Taiwan, the horses can have skin diseases.

Regarding the age of a horse, the expert mentions an estimate of a horse's age can be made from looking at its teeth. However, this is just a very rough estimate. It’s better to estimate the coat or figure. An old horse’s coat becomes dull, and ribs showing. In general, a horse in its twenties equals to a human in his sixties. Therefore, the coat certainly becomes duller than before.

After finishing topic of the horses’ age, we ask the expert another funny question “Do they need to brush their teeth?” All of sudden, the expert brusted into laughing, and then answered “That’s such a joke. Horses never brush their teeth. By chewing the grass and drinking water, they can clean their teeth. For the most part, horses don’t have many problems with their teeth.

During our interview, we saw one of the horses lay down to sleep like a cow. It’s extremely interesting to us. As to how to build strong relationships with the horses, the expert told us he educates the horses with love, like educating a child. Appropriate encouragements are essential, such as offering grass, gently patting. If you hit a horse, it would get far away from you. It’s not because of hatred, but fear.

Later, the expert wanted to introduce a special carriage, so he led us to a warehouse. Upon entering the place, we saw there is a big carriage, which is American import, handmade, and wooden. How beautiful it is! We were very excited, asked for sitting on the soft seat, and took several pictures. According to the expert, the carriage was used in wedding for brides and grooms before.

Furthermore, we also took a look at a tool room. It’s another big place which contains medicine, vitamins, forages, etc. These are all for the horses. Everything proves that how professional the expert is! He showed imported Bermuda hay and hay bags to us. The importance of the hay bags is beyond our imagination. As a matter of fact, horses eat only clean hay!
Afterwards, we also saw the medicine, such as hypodermic needles, syringes, and so on. Most medicine horses use is the same as human beings’. How surprising! The only difference is that horses are injected more substances into their body than humans. For example, the Breton weighs roughly 800 kg, and it must use 8 injection bottles. Also, it is given a shot by a bigger hypodermic needle. Hearing that, we all were terrified out of wits.

The final part is about shoeing. In hot shoeing, the shoe is heated on the spot. It is then hammered into shape and placed on the foot for a few seconds to char a brown rim on the hoof. The shoe can then be altered if necessary, and so can the hoof. Later, the calks or toe grabs can be forged on their feet. A horseshoe should be held securely by nails that are hammered through the horny part of the foot. It seems painful for us!
It’s a fun day for all of us, especially when we rode the mini horses. The expert allowed us to ride one of the mini horses. Riding on the horseback, we were filled with excitement, joys in our hearts. There was something funny to share with you. One of our members rode it with her body oblique; while the other one was almost too chubby for the horse to carry him.
