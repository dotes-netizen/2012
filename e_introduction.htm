Introduction
White Horse Farm, which includes both White Horse Wineland Resort and Lin’s Stud Farm, runs with complex management. It expects to be the local feature and increate business opportunities for the country. The wine shop produces natural liquors such as Kaoliang and grains liquor without adding any flavorings and health-preserving liqueurs such as grapes, mulberries and garlic wine. The stud farm focuses on breeding miniature horses and provides the experience of riding a horse. It also offers visitors activities of preserved eggs and salted duck eggs DIY.
Along the Coast of Hsienhsi Township, you can see the clear blue sky with clouds, the beautiful beach, and green buildings. It attracts many visitors and even some brides and grooms come here to take wedding photos! It can help you relax your mind and feel good. Such beautiful scenic spots are worth a visit. (Note 1)
