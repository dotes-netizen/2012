In the early time, White Horse Farm is Lin’s Stud Farm. Therefore, you can’t miss riding a horse when you visit here.
The owner picked a gentle white horse because a horse with a sweet temper is safer for visitors to ride.
First, put saddle over the horse’s back.
Tie the saddle tightly and prepare to go.
Let the horse warm up by walking around the yard.
Straddle the horse and sit on its back.
It’s cool to ride a horse in the yard.
If children are afraid of riding a horsing, it is also interesting for them to buy some grass to feed the horses.
To be careful and not be bitten by the horse.

Postscript:
The weather is fine today; the owner leaded a little white horse to let us experience how to ride. Strictly speaking, we just sat on the horse rather than rode it because the horse didn’t run. It just walked in the yard.
Run! Walk! Go! We do exercise in the horse riding zone. It’s romantic to ride a horse in such beautiful scenery. However, it’s not easy to sit on the horse back because we should chin up and chest out to keep our balance, or we might fall from the horse.
Besides horse riding, the owner also leaded the little white horse to the lawn to let us take the photos of riding. He also lets us lead it to eat grass. The little horse is so calm that it was willing to follow us. The experience is very special and interesting.
