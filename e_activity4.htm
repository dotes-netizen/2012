The owner cooperates with Lovely Moms’ Workroom in Shengang Township to increase some activities for parents and kids. They invite a handicraft teacher to teach people to make card cases, frog pens, drift wood wind chimes, and seashell wind chimes. All the works are made of seashells. The teacher proudly says that all of them are environment-friendly works.

The teacher was explaining how to make it.

The teacher taught every visitor carefully.

A big family was making the handicrafts when we went to interview today. These works can be completed for only thirty minutes. Furthermore, they are beautiful but functional.
Card case: You can decorate as you wish with seashells.
Frog pen: Seashells pieced up a very cute frog and there’s a pen in the shape of a lotus leaf set next to it. That makes the work more vivid and lovely.
Draft wood wind chime: The upper pillar is a piece of draft wood and the decoration is strung with seashells.
Seashell wind chime: The chime is made of all sizes of seashell strings and you can write some wishes on the big seashell.


Postscript:
What I made was a frog pen. It becomes cuter because I made it myself! It didn’t take me much time to make it. The materials required are as follows: 6 shells of the same color department, two big round rolling eyes, a block of wood with a hole on it, a ball-point pen which can be inserted into the wood block, and hot melt adhesive manufacturing method: squeeze some hot melt adhesive in the hole of the wood block, and stick in the cap of the pen. Then we would make the frog: first of all, we picked a shell to stick it on the wood block(for the feet), then picked two similar shells to make mouth, and then picked a shell for the body and stuck the rest two shells on the both side of the body of the frog for the hands. After that, the work was done! The work we made today can beautify the house and can provide us pens to make records. It’s not only beautiful but also practical.
